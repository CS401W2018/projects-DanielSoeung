What is the World Cup?

Soccer is a sport has grew into a huge favor across the globe. Each country with its unique talents coming to the sport makes the sport just that much exciting. The purpose of the World Cup is to bring unity for the world by having different teams from across the world play against each other to see who is the best nation in soccer. The event is a huge deal across the world, benefiting local economies, viewship records and discovering new and exciting talent. This event is truly an event you cannot miss.

How does the brackets work?

FIFA has divided the qaulification into different continents each with limited spots below are the list of ways countries can qualify for the FIFA World Cup with their respective contents.

  1. AFC (8) Asian Football Confederation
  2. CAF (9) Confederation of African Football
  3. Concacaf (6) Confederation of North, Central America and Caribbean Association
  4. CONMEBOL (6) Confederación Sudamericana de Fútbol
