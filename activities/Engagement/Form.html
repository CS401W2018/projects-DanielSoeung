Year in School:

The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

The action attribute tells the form what to do. Like redirect it to another page

The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

Method is what you want to the form to do. You want it to POST the data or GET the data.

What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

POST is when you are changing data on the webiste and GET is when you need it to read your input.

What are different ways to style your form?

You can use float, grid and flex.

Why does the professor like to use flex?

Flex allows for the website to look great for both desktop and mobile devices
