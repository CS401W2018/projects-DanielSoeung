What is normal flow in HTML?

Normal is the layout of the page or how elements are placed

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

The float create a space where elements can share space. If you do not want an element to share a space then you would clear the float so the property stops applying to it

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

Absoulte will scroll with the page. Fixed will stay in the same position as you scroll the page. Relative allows you to slide the image or element in the spaces reserved for it.
