Graves is a champion from League of Legends. His ability to burst down enemy champions makes him the ideal pick for important targets that need to be taken down. Toegther with his double barrel shotgun and critical strike builds, Graves is considered a lethal champion to play against.

In the League of Legends lore Graves is consider to be a mercenary and a thief in many city. His ability to wield his double barrel shotgun makes all who cross his path think twice if they desire to hurt Graves. Despite his criminal behavior, in the lore Graves is acutally considered to be good, often seem fighting with Demacians or other good characters.
